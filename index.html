Location & Parking

Little Shop of Stories Parking in Decatur, GA Detailed Parking Map

Our advice...

Grab a metered parking space if you can! Metered parking in the city is free on Sundays. If that doesn't work out, head over to the Courthouse Deck (see parking map). It's a bit of a walk, but you'll save money that way. If someone in your party needs to be dropped off, you can pull through the metered drive just in front of Little Shop and let your passenger(s) out there!

If you want to park close, and there are no metered spots available, you can park in the Town Center Deck, just across E. Ponce, for $10 (flat rate).
